MOBILE STRATEGY IMPERATIVES
  • Landing Page UX: Must be flawless on mobile. Forms need to be short (3-5 fields max)
  • Page Speed: Mobile users won't wait. Target under 2 second load time
  • CTA Placement: Above the fold, large tap targets, clear action buttons
  • Image Optimization: High-quality product images that load fast on mobile networks
  • Ad Copy: Written for mobile screens - shorter headlines, punchy descriptions
  • Future Bid Adjustments: Consider +15-20% mobile bid modifier once campaigns mature
MOBILE CONVERSION BARRIERS TO AUDIT
  • Form fields requiring excessive typing on mobile keyboard
  • Small tap targets causing mis-clicks and frustration
  • Slow-loading product images eating mobile data
  • Multi-step processes losing users between pages
  • Phone number fields without auto-format
  • Pop-ups blocking content on small screens
