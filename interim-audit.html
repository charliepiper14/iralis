  • Landing Page UX: Must be flawless on mobile. Forms need to be short (3-5 fields max)

  • Form fields requiring excessive typing on mobile keyboard

  • Slow-loading product images eating mobile data
  • Multi-step processes losing users between pages
  • Phone number fields without auto-format
  • Pop-ups blocking content on small screens
